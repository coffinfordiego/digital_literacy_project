# Social Media Checklist for College Students

Being a Digital Ambassador means leading by example. Use this checklist to maintain a professional and responsible digital footprint.

### The Do's
* **Do** audit your privacy settings regularly to ensure your personal posts are only visible to friends and family.
* **Do** use platforms like LinkedIn to network with alumni, industry professionals, and potential employers.
* **Do** share your academic achievements, volunteer work, and professional milestones to build a positive "digital brand."
* **Do** think twice before posting; consider if you would be comfortable with a professor or future boss seeing the content.
* **Do** engage in constructive and respectful digital discourse, even when you disagree with others' opinions.

### The Don'ts
* **Don't** post photos or videos that depict illegal activities, substance abuse, or unprofessional behavior.
* **Don't** use social media as a place to vent about specific professors, classes, or current employers.
* **Don't** share sensitive personal details such as your home address, student ID number, or current precise location.
* **Don't** engage in "cyber-bullying" or heated public arguments that could be traced back to your professional identity.
* **Don't** post confidential information about your school, internships, or workplace projects.
